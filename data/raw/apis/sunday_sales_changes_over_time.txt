Bans on Off-Premises Sunday Sales
=================================

Laws banning Sunday sales of alcoholic beverages for off-premises consumption as of January 1, 1998 and repeals from that date forward.

Click “Download/Export” to download the policy data to a spreadsheet or CSV file. Use the tabs above to select the main display options (Policies on a Specific Date; Policy Changes Over Time; Timeline of Changes) and to access related information (Maps & Charts; Variables definitions; Policy Description and further information).
